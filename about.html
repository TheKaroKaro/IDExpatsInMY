About This Directory

Community-driven directory for Indonesian expats in Malaysia

Our Mission

To create a reliable, community-verified network of service providers for Indonesian expats living in Malaysia. We believe in the power of shared experiences to build trust and make life abroad easier.

How It Works

1

Community Submission

Expats submit contacts they have personally used and trust

2

Verification Process

We verify each submission through multiple community sources and cross-checking

3

Publication

Verified contacts are published with community reviews and ratings

Community Guidelines

Authentic Experiences

Only submit contacts you have personally used

Honest Reviews

Share both positive and constructive experiences

Privacy First

We never share your personal information

Respectful Engagement

Maintain respectful discussions in comments

Important Disclaimer

This directory is created and maintained by the community for informational purposes only. While we verify contacts to the best of our ability, we cannot guarantee their services. Always exercise due diligence and use your own judgment when engaging with any service provider.
