About This Directory

Our Mission

Moving to a new country can be challenging. Finding reliable service providers shouldn't be one of them. This directory was created by expats for expats, to share trusted contacts that have been personally verified by our community.

How It Works

1

Community Submissions

Expats submit contacts they've personally used and trust

2

Manual Verification

Our team contacts each submission to verify details

3

Publication

Verified contacts are added to the public directory

4

Community Feedback

Users can leave comments and ratings based on their experiences

Submission & Approval Process

Timeline

  • Submission: Immediate via our form
  • Initial Review: 1-2 business days
  • Verification Call: 1-3 business days
  • Publication: 3-5 business days total

Review Criteria

  • Contact information accuracy
  • Service relevance to expat needs
  • Professionalism and responsiveness
  • No conflicts of interest
  • No self-promotion from service providers
